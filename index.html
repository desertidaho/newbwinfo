About

Brett is an experienced full-stack software developer. Brett grew up in Idaho and graduated magna cum laude from Boise State University with B.A. in philosophy and minors in economics and business. Prior to software development Brett had a 20 year career in aviation working as a commercial helicopter pilot flying single pilot missions in support of fire, heliski, and geophysical exploration missions, as well as time in the Navy flying in the Viking jet aircraft.
